Privacy Policy for Facebook Reel Upload App

Last updated: March 12, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Facebook Reel Upload Application ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Our app requires access to your Facebook Page to upload reels on your behalf. We collect:

  • Basic profile information to identify your Facebook Page
  • Page access tokens to authorize uploads
  • Content you choose to upload as reels

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity with Facebook
  • Upload video content to your Facebook Page as requested
  • Manage the publishing process of your reels

Data Storage and Security

We do not permanently store your Facebook access tokens or uploaded content. All data is processed locally on your device and only transmitted securely to Facebook's servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with Facebook's Graph API to upload content. Please refer to Facebook's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke our app's access to your Facebook Page at any time through Facebook's settings
  • Request information about what data we process
  • Request deletion of any stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
